About Zplit

Built for the
decentralized future

Zplit is a collaborative project between AOSSIE and StabilityNexus, bringing together expertise in open-source software and decentralized technologies.

We believe in putting users first - that's why Zplit is built on principles of privacy, transparency, and user empowerment. No central servers collecting your data, no tracking your spending habits, just pure peer-to-peer expense management.
